You will need:

  1. Graham Crackers
  2. Hershey's Chocolate
  3. Large Marshmellows
  4. A heat source (campfire, stovetop, or microwave)

  1. Heat up the marshmellow in whichever way you have a heat source for.
  2. Once heated to desired effect, stack the chocolate on top of the graham crackers
  3. Put the warm marshmellow on top of the chocolate
  4. Stack the last cracker on top of the whole ensemble
  5. Enjoy!

Once you follow these steps, you'll have a delicious treat to enjoy with the whole family!
