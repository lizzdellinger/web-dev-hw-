Why S'mores?

I chose to talk about this activity of making s'mores because it's something that has been in my family for a very long time. Sitting around a campfire and making s'mores has always been an important part of my childhood and I'll always remember it.

In order to find and order the supplies you will need to make s'mores, visit your local Walmart store!
